About the Project

Mathmax is a mobile app which features multiplayer Maths quiz game upto 4 players at a time like a group match online.
My primary aim was to build a clean ui which will be easily usable across android as well as iOS devices. The overall ui must be look simple and not having any complex steps to play with it. Users can play as guest and if they want to participate in global score board they need to signup or login using google account with simple steps..
